• What is the test format?
The exam consists of five free response questions comprised of multiple parts. Please write clear solutions in your responses. You must show work in order to receive credit.
The questions are written to span a wide variety of topics. Remember that all competitors are expected to uphold to a code of honor. It is prohibited to discuss the problems with other competitors including sharing responses or collaborating. However, resources such as textbooks and Wikipedia are available for use during the exam.

• How do I submit my work?
Please send your responses to torreychemistry@gmail.com. Accepted forms include files with .pdf, .txt, and .doc/x extensions.
